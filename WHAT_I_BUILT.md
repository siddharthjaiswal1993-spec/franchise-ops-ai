## Key Design Decisions Encoded in the Docs

**The DETECT → DECIDE → ACT → VERIFY loop as the central narrative** — not a feature list, but a complete operational workflow that the platform owns end-to-end.

**Location Health Score as the product anchor** — a single, explainable, predictive score gives franchisors and operators a shared language for location performance that replaces 15 separate data reports.

**AI Field Consultant as the wedge** — the field consulting workflow is the highest-frequency, highest-value operational problem that has the clearest AI augmentation story. It is the right first problem to solve before expanding to portfolio intelligence.
