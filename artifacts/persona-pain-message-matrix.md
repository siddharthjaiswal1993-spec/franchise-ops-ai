# Persona-Pain-Message Matrix

> A concise mapping of persona, primary pain, core message, relevant module, key metric, and buying trigger for 13 franchise operations stakeholders.

---

| Persona | Primary Pain | Core Message | Product Module | Key Metric They Own | Buying Trigger |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **CEO / Founder** | No real-time portfolio health view; operational crises discovered too late | "Real-time portfolio health across every location in your system — not compiled monthly, updated continuously." | Executive Command Center | AUV trend, brand compliance rate, franchisee satisfaction | Preparing for PE raise or sale; public audit failure; franchise system growth crossing 200 locations |
| **COO / Head of Operations** | Field consulting is under-tooled; corrective action closure rates are low; no way to measure whether interventions work | "Operational intelligence that tells you which locations need attention this week — and verifies that interventions actually worked." | Field Consultant Workspace + Location Health Score | Corrective action closure rate, field visit completion rate, operational consistency index | New COO hire with 90-day mandate; field consultant team growing beyond current tools; board asking for operational risk metrics |
| **VP of Franchise Operations** | Data in 6 disconnected systems; monthly report compilation takes days; no objective framework for visit prioritization | "One view of every location's operational health — updated continuously, not compiled manually every month." | Location Health Score + Risk and Alerts Engine | Operations consistency score, time-to-corrective-action-closure, visit completion vs. schedule | VP hired to modernize operations function; franchise system crossing 100 locations; COO requesting data-driven prioritization |
| **Director of Field Operations** | FCs spend 35% of time on admin; visit quality is inconsistent and unmeasured; no way to measure field consulting ROI | "Pre-visit briefs in 30 seconds. Visit notes converted in real time. Corrective action plans drafted before leaving the parking lot." | AI Field Consultant + Visit Intelligence | Visit completion rate, corrective action closure rate post-visit, FC admin time consumed | FC team expansion; COO requesting proof of field consulting ROI; inconsistent visit quality raised in leadership review |
| **Director of Training** | Training completion rates below 70%; no connection between training completion and performance; high turnover creates constant onboarding burden | "Connect training completions to location performance outcomes — see which training modules actually improve operational scores." | LMS Integration + Location Health Score (training dimension) | Training completion rate, certification currency rate, training-to-performance correlation | Compliance training audit finding gaps; leadership asking for training ROI data; turnover spike requiring urgent onboarding improvement |
| **Compliance Manager** | Static audit schedules ignore risk; 40% of corrective actions never close; no predictive signal between audits | "Risk-stratified compliance — your highest-risk locations audited more frequently, with AI-generated corrective action plans that actually get closed." | Compliance and Audit Copilot + Closed-Loop Actions | Audit pass rate, corrective action closure rate, repeat violation rate | Audit failure pattern creating regulatory risk; franchise system expansion exceeding compliance team capacity; legal review of corrective action backlog |
| **Franchise Development Leader** | Franchisee success is hard to predict; development-to-operations handoff is fragmented; candidates want proof of support | "Show franchise candidates exactly how your system supports their success — with operational data, not promises." | CRM Integration + Location Health Score | New unit openings/year, franchisee success rate (Year 1-3), time-to-open | Franchisee failure rate increasing; PE pressure to improve unit economics; franchise candidates asking about operational support |
| **Franchisee / MUO** | No peer benchmarking visibility; compliance feels punitive; HQ support is slow and reactive | "Know exactly where your location stands — and exactly what actions will improve your performance — before HQ calls." | Operator Command Center + Location Health Score (franchisee view) + Franchise Support Desk | Location AUV, labor cost %, audit score, guest satisfaction | Portfolio growing beyond 15 locations; audit failure creating urgency; new professional operations hire |
| **Store Manager** | Can't quickly find SOP answers during busy shifts; task reporting is time-consuming; new employee onboarding is inconsistent | "Ask any operational question in plain language and get an instant answer sourced from your brand's own SOPs." | Franchise Support Desk + Task Management | Shift completion score, food safety compliance, task completion rate | Upcoming health inspection; new franchise owner rollout; manager flagged for SOP non-compliance |
| **Frontline Employee** | Training feels like a compliance burden, not a learning opportunity; unclear what's expected on shift | "Training that fits into your shift, not on top of it." | LMS (mobile training) + Task Management | Training completion rate, certification currency | Franchisee rolling out new platform; compliance training deadline approaching |
| **CFO / Finance Leader** | Royalty reconciliation is partially manual; franchisee financial distress discovered too late; PE board requires EBITDA-level location data | "Royalty anomaly detection and franchisee financial distress signals — surfaced before royalties go delinquent." | Executive Command Center (financial) + Data and Integrations Layer | Royalty collection rate, franchisee financial health index, AUV distribution | PE board request for enhanced financial reporting; royalty delinquency incident; audit preparation |
| **CMO / Marketing Leader** | Brand compliance at local marketing level is hard to monitor; co-op fund management is administrative overhead | "Customer experience signals from reviews and ratings — in the same platform as your operational compliance data." | Location Health Score (customer experience dimension) + Benchmarking | Brand compliance score, co-op fund utilization, review rating trends | Brand incident from non-compliant local marketing; co-op fund management breaking at scale |
| **PE Operating Partner** | No cross-portfolio operational intelligence view; underperforming assets identified too late; LP reporting requires manual aggregation | "Portfolio-level operational intelligence across every brand, every region, and every location — in one view." | Executive Command Center + Location Health Score + Risk and Alerts Engine | Portfolio EBITDA trend, health score distribution, AUV variance | New portfolio company acquisition; LP reporting cycle; portfolio company underperforming EBITDA targets |
