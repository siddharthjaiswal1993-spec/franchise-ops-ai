## High-Value Module Combinations by Persona

**For COO/CEO:** Executive Command Center + Location Health Score + Risk and Alerts Engine = full portfolio intelligence view. This combination makes the platform indispensable at the C-suite level.

**For Director of Field Operations:** Field Consultant Workspace + Visit Intelligence + Closed-Loop Actions = complete field consulting transformation. This combination eliminates administrative burden and creates measurable field consulting ROI.

**For Compliance Manager:** Compliance and Audit Intelligence + Closed-Loop Actions + Governance and Trust Layer = proactive compliance management with full auditability. Strong regulatory risk reduction story.

**For Multi-Unit Operators:** Operator Command Center + Benchmarking + Closed-Loop Actions = portfolio intelligence purpose-built for MUO needs. Direct-to-MUO packaging opportunity.

**For PE Operating Partners:** Executive Command Center + Location Health Score + Benchmarking = cross-portfolio intelligence with cross-brand comparative data. Highest-value combination for PE operating partner channel.
