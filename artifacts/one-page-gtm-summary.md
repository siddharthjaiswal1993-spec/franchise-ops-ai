# One-Page GTM Summary: AI-Native Franchise Operational Intelligence

---

## Market Thesis

Franchise operations software is transitioning from workflow digitization (systems of record) to operational intelligence (systems of intelligence). The winning platform will close the loop from signal to verified action — detecting risk, recommending interventions, routing action, and verifying outcomes across the full franchise portfolio. This is a 3-5x revenue per customer expansion opportunity for the platform that defines the new category.

---

## Ideal Customer Profile

**Primary:** Mid-market service-heavy franchise brands, 50-500 locations, QSR / home services / fitness / personal care verticals. VP of Operations as champion; COO/CEO as economic buyer. Field consultant team of 5-30. $100K-$500K annual software budget. Pain: field consulting is under-tooled, corrective actions don't close, risk is discovered too late.

**Secondary:** PE-backed franchise brands (any size); multi-unit operators (5-100 locations, direct buyer).

---

## Primary Wedge

**AI Field Consultant** — 8-week funded pilot ($15K-$25K), 10 field consultants, 30 locations. ROI: 8+ hours/week admin recaptured per FC; corrective action closure rate +20+ points; time-to-summary reduced 80%+. Data created by the pilot powers the Location Health Score expansion.

## Pricing Principle

Per-location pricing (not per-user). Foundation Tier: $8-15/location/month. Intelligence Tier: $45-80/location/month. Unlimited frontline users at every tier. Expansion drives 3-5x revenue per customer over 24 months.

## 3 GTM Motions

**1. Pilot-first sales motion:** Consultative discovery → funded pilot → ROI evidence → full contract. Target: 70%+ pilot-to-contract conversion.

**2. PE operating partner channel:** Invitation-only roundtable program → portfolio company evaluations → multi-brand deployments. One PE relationship = 3-5 simultaneous evaluations.

**3. Content and category creation:** Annual Franchise Operations Benchmark Report + maturity assessment + IFA thought leadership → defines "franchise operational intelligence" category before competitors can name it.

## Top 3 Risks

1. **Integration delay risk:** AI quality depends on integration depth. Mitigate by requiring minimum integration stack before pilot launch and pre-integrating before Day 1.

2. **Field consultant adoption risk:** Change management is a cultural challenge. Mitigate by framing as admin burden elimination, not workflow monitoring; identify FC champions early.

3. **Category timing risk:** If FranConnect moves slowly, AI-native entrants will define the category. Mitigate by prioritizing category narrative investment alongside product investment.

---

## Recommendation

Move on the AI Field Consultant wedge in the next 90 days with a pilot-first GTM motion. Simultaneously invest in category creation marketing (benchmark report, maturity model, IFA positioning). The product and marketing investments are parallel and mutually reinforcing. Both require urgency — the window is 18-24 months.
