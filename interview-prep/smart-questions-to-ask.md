# Smart Questions to Ask: FranConnect Interviews

> 20 questions demonstrating strategic depth and genuine curiosity. Organized by role.

---

## Questions for the Director of Marketing

1. **Category narrative:** "How is FranConnect currently positioning in the market — is there an explicit effort to define a new category around operational intelligence, or is the current messaging more focused on feature differentiation? And how is that resonating with the mid-market ICP?"

2. **Demand generation maturity:** "What does the current demand generation engine look like — what's the mix between content/inbound and outbound/ABM, and which channels are currently producing the highest-quality pipeline? Where are the biggest conversion gaps?"

3. **AI story readiness:** "How is the team currently communicating the AI roadmap to the market? Is there a clear narrative for what AI-native franchise intelligence means, or is that a message that still needs to be built?"

4. **Thought leadership positioning:** "What is FranConnect's current presence at IFA and MUFC? Is there an established speaking or thought leadership program, or is that an area that needs to be developed?"

5. **Competitive messaging:** "How often are Delightree and Naranga coming up in competitive evaluations? Is the team finding that buyers are primarily evaluating FranConnect against direct competitors, or more often against the status quo of their current toolset?"

6. **Customer evidence strategy:** "What is the current case study and customer story infrastructure like — is there a systematic process for capturing quantified customer outcomes, or is it more ad hoc?"

7. **Marketing team structure:** "How is the marketing team currently structured — where is the split between content/brand, demand generation, product marketing, and events? And where are the biggest talent gaps?"

8. **PE segment strategy:** "Is there a specific marketing strategy for the PE-backed franchisor and PE operating partner segment? That segment is a high-value ICP with distinct buying triggers. Is it currently treated as a separate campaign focus?"

---

## Questions for the VP of Product

9. **AI architecture status:** "Where does the Location Health Score stand in the current product roadmap — is it built, in pilot, or still in design? And what is the primary technical constraint on the timeline for a customer-facing deployment?"

10. **Integration depth:** "What is the current average number of integrations per active customer, and which integration sources are most commonly connected? Is POS integration available today, and if so, what's the adoption rate?"

11. **Data model:** "How is the franchise-native data model structured today — specifically, how does the platform handle the brand-region-district-location hierarchy, and how role-aware is the current data access architecture?"

12. **Build vs. buy philosophy:** "How is the product team thinking about the AI build vs. buy decision? Specifically, is the current direction to build the intelligence models in-house, or to use third-party AI platforms and focus on the franchise-native layer?"

13. **Trust architecture:** "What is the current state of the governance infrastructure for AI recommendations — is there a source citation and confidence scoring system in place, and how are human approval gates designed into the current or planned AI workflows?"

14. **Wedge sequencing:** "Is there internal alignment on the AI Field Consultant as the primary product wedge? Or is there discussion about leading with a different entry point — health score, compliance copilot, or something else?"

15. **Competitive intelligence:** "From a product strategy perspective, how are you monitoring the Delightree and new AI entrant threat? Is the team doing structured competitive intelligence on their product roadmap?"

16. **Customer feedback loop:** "How does customer feedback from operations leaders — COOs, VPs of Operations, field consultants — get into the product roadmap process? Is there a structured advisory board or regular voice-of-customer program?"

---

## Questions for Either Role (Strategic / Both)

17. **FranConnect's self-assessment:** "Where does FranConnect currently sit on the systems-of-record to systems-of-intelligence evolution curve? Is the internal view that FranConnect is in Stage 2 (workflow), Stage 3 (execution), or beginning Stage 4 (intelligence)?"

18. **Internal alignment:** "How aligned is the leadership team on the AI intelligence platform vision? Is there strong internal conviction that this is the right strategic direction, or is there healthy internal debate about the pace of change?"

19. **The 18-24 month bet:** "If FranConnect gets this right in the next 18-24 months — builds the AI Field Consultant wedge, establishes the Location Health Score as the industry standard, and defines the franchise operational intelligence category — what does the business look like at the end of that period? What is the size prize?"

20. **What good looks like:** "What would make a Director of Marketing / VP of Product at FranConnect genuinely excellent in this role — not just competent, but transformational? What would you most want to see from the right person in this position?"
