## What's Exemplary

**The DETECT → DECIDE → ACT → VERIFY loop as the central organising principle.** This is the right way to frame operational intelligence. It explains why point-solution competitors (audit tools, task management platforms) fail to deliver the full value — they own one stage of the loop, not all four. This is a genuine strategic insight, not a marketing position.

**The Location Health Score framework.** A composite, explainable, predictive score per location — combining operational, financial, compliance, and customer signals — is the right product anchor. It creates a single number that franchisors and operators can build operational workflows around, rather than managing 15 separate data streams.

**The wedge strategy.** Starting with AI Field Consultant (high-frequency, high-value operational workflow) before expanding to portfolio intelligence is the right GTM sequencing. The field consulting problem has clear ROI and minimal procurement complexity. Portfolio intelligence requires more data maturity and multi-stakeholder buy-in.

**The research paper depth.** The paper articulates a category thesis — not just a product pitch — with market structure analysis, competitive dynamics, and the strategic rationale for why now. This is the level of thinking that drives category-level conversations.

---

## What Would Come Next

**Integration specifications.** The platform needs to connect to franchise management systems (FranConnect, Salesforce, Yext), POS systems, and compliance tracking tools. The data ingestion and normalisation challenge across heterogeneous franchise tech stacks is the hardest technical problem.

**Franchisor vs. franchisee value proposition.** The product serves both, but their interests are not always aligned. A clearer articulation of what each party gets — and how conflicts are resolved — would strengthen the go-to-market story.

**Pricing model specificity.** The GTM strategy covers pricing approach but would benefit from a fuller unit economics model: ACV, expansion triggers, MSP / master franchisee tier pricing.
