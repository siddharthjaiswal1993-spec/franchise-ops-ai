# 90-Day GTM Plan: AI-Native Franchise Operational Intelligence

---

## Overview

The 90-day GTM plan is organized around three phases: Foundation (Days 1-30), Validation (Days 31-60), and Scale (Days 61-90). Each phase builds on the previous and ends with a clear go/no-go decision point.

The plan assumes the AI Field Consultant product module is either available or in late development, and that the primary GTM motion is pilot-first: close a funded 8-week AI Field Consultant pilot with 2-3 existing customers, generate ROI evidence, and use that evidence to accelerate mid-market pipeline.

---

## Phase 1: Foundation (Days 1-30)

### Objectives

1. Validate and sharpen the ICP hypothesis with market evidence — not just internal assumption
2. Establish the competitive intelligence baseline that will inform messaging and positioning
3. Close 2 funded AI Field Consultant pilots with existing customers

### Key Actions

**Action 1: ICP Validation Interviews (Days 1-14)**
Schedule 12-15 structured discovery interviews with franchise operations leaders across the target segments: 4-5 mid-market QSR brands, 3-4 home services brands, 2-3 PE-backed brands, 2-3 MUOs. Validate: pain intensity for field consulting problem, current toolset, budget for AI intelligence tools, decision-making structure, willingness to pilot.

Deliverable: ICP validation report — confirm or adjust target segment prioritization.

**Action 2: Competitive Audit (Days 1-14, parallel)**
Deploy structured competitive intelligence collection: direct product evaluations of Delightree, Naranga, and BrandWide; win/loss analysis of last 20 deals; analysis of competitor messaging, positioning, and customer reviews on G2 and Capterra.

Deliverable: Competitive positioning brief — where to attack, where to cede, which objections to prepare for.

**Action 3: Message Testing (Days 15-21)**
Test 3 message hypotheses in market: (a) "AI Field Consultant" productivity message to Director of Field Operations; (b) "Location Health Score" risk intelligence message to COO/VP Operations; (c) "Systems of intelligence" category message to CEO/COO.

Method: 6 LinkedIn sponsored posts with controlled variants; 5 follow-up calls with message-qualified prospects; sales team feedback on discovery call resonance.

Deliverable: Validated messaging hierarchy — which messages create the most urgency by persona.

**Action 4: Pilot Design Finalization (Days 1-10)**
Finalize the AI Field Consultant pilot structure: 8-week duration, 10 field consultants, 30 locations, specific baseline metrics, measurement framework, ROI calculation methodology. Prepare pilot proposal document and pricing ($15,000-$25,000 funded pilot).

**Action 5: Pilot Close with 2 Existing Customers (Days 10-30)**
Identify 3-5 existing customers who are good pilot candidates (active field consultant team, 50+ locations, operations-focused leadership, POS integration available). Present pilot proposal in customer success calls. Close 2 funded pilots by Day 30.

Deliverable: 2 signed pilot agreements.

## Phase 2: Validation (Days 31-60)

### Objectives

1. Execute pilots and capture quantified ROI evidence — the business case for the full platform sale
2. Refine messaging based on pilot observation and customer language
3. Launch pipeline generation to build the mid-market funnel

### Key Actions

**Action 1: Pilot Execution (Days 31-60)**
Both pilots are running in full execution mode. Customer Success manages onboarding, integration setup, and weekly check-ins. Week 3-6 are live AI-assisted field visits. Week 7-8 are ROI measurement and case study preparation.

Key measurement moments: baseline capture (Week 1-2), midpoint check (Week 4), final measurement (Week 7-8). Capture: hours of admin time recaptured per FC per week; corrective action closure rate vs. pre-pilot baseline; time-to-visit-summary submission; field consultant satisfaction with pre-visit briefs; number of AI recommendations reviewed and accepted vs. overridden.

**Action 2: ROI Documentation and Case Study Creation (Days 50-60)**
Before pilots complete, begin drafting the case study framework: situation, problem, solution, and — critically — quantified outcomes. Target 3+ quantified metrics per case study. Prepare executive summary versions (1 page) and full versions (4-6 pages). Get customer approval for public use.

Deliverable: 2 approved case studies with quantified outcomes.

**Action 3: Message Refinement (Days 31-45)**
Based on pilot observation and customer language captured in pilot check-in calls, refine the messaging framework. Field consultants will use specific language to describe what the AI does for them that may be more compelling than the language in the current messaging framework. Capture it.

Update positioning and messaging document. Brief sales team on refined language.

**Action 4: Pipeline Generation Launch (Days 35-60)**
Launch the demand generation engine in parallel with pilot execution:
- LinkedIn sponsored content: 3 posts per week using validated messaging, targeting VP Operations and Director of Field Operations at mid-market franchise brands
- Maturity assessment: Deploy the Franchise Operational Maturity Assessment tool; drive traffic via LinkedIn and email
- Benchmark report: Publish a preliminary benchmark data piece based on available platform data ("Franchise Operations Benchmarks: 2026 Preliminary Data")
- Cold outreach: Sales development rep cold outreach to 100 mid-market franchise brands in ICP segments using a short, specific message tied to field consulting pain

Target: 30+ qualified demos booked in Phase 2.

**Action 5: Franchise Consultant Partner Outreach (Days 40-60)**
Begin outreach to the top 25 franchise consultants in the partner program target list. Present partner program structure and referral economics. Offer co-marketing opportunity (joint webinar). Target: 10 partner conversations; 5 partner agreements.

## Phase 3: Scale (Days 61-90)

### Objectives

1. Expand pilots to 5 brands (adding 3 new pilot deployments from pipeline built in Phase 2)
2. Launch full demand generation with case study evidence as proof point
3. Refine pricing based on pilot data and validate expansion triggers
4. Build the PE operating partner channel

### Key Actions

**Action 1: Convert Phase 2 Pilots to Full Contracts and Close 3 New Pilots (Days 61-75)**
With 2 case studies in hand, convert both Phase 2 pilot customers to full platform contracts. Use the case study evidence to accelerate 3 new pilot closes from the Phase 2 pipeline of 30+ qualified demos.

Target: 2 pilot-to-contract conversions + 3 new pilot closes = 5 total active pilots or contracts.

**Action 2: Full Demand Generation Launch (Days 65-90)**
With case study evidence and validated messaging, launch the full demand generation engine:
- Paid LinkedIn: 5 posts per week; A/B test case study evidence angle vs. benchmark data angle
- Email: Monthly newsletter to prospect and customer list; personalized sequences from SDRs to ICP accounts
- Content: First full benchmark report draft; first ROI case study video
- Webinar: Launch "Franchise Intelligence Quarterly" webinar series (first episode: "What Field Consulting Looks Like in an AI-Native Franchise System")
- IFA planning: Secure speaking slot at next IFA Annual Convention; design conference presence around Location Health Score demo

**Action 3: Pricing Validation (Days 61-75)**
With pilot ROI data in hand, validate the Intelligence tier price point with 5 customer conversations: "If the pilot results continue to hold — X hours per FC recaptured, Y% improvement in corrective action closure rates — what would the right annual investment be for this capability at your scale?"

Use responses to calibrate the Intelligence tier price range and ensure the ROI-to-price ratio creates a straightforward buying decision.

**Action 4: Define Expansion Contract Triggers (Days 70-85)**
Document the specific conditions that should trigger a customer expansion conversation: from Operations Tier to Intelligence Tier; from Field Consultant module to Location Health Score; from Health Score to Compliance Copilot; from Compliance to Executive Intelligence Layer.

Create a Customer Success playbook for expansion triggers. Ensure Customer Success is monitoring health score adoption and creating expansion opportunities systematically.

**Action 5: PE Channel Development (Days 61-90)**
Identify 10 PE firms with franchise portfolio exposure (QSR, home services, fitness focus). Research the operating partner or portfolio management contact at each firm. Create personalized outreach using a specific operational intelligence angle ("cross-portfolio benchmarking for your franchise portfolio companies"). Target: 5 initial conversations, 2 roundtable invitations, 1 portfolio evaluation initiated.

**Action 6: Partner Program Launch (Days 75-90)**
Formally launch the franchise consultant partner program: publish partner portal, send launch announcement to all engaged consultants, begin tracking referred pipeline.

## 90-Day Success Summary

| Phase | Key Milestone | Success Indicator |
|---|---|---|
| Day 30 | 2 funded pilots signed | Confidence in pilot model and ICP accuracy |
| Day 60 | 2 case studies drafted with quantified outcomes | Confidence in AI Field Consultant ROI story |
| Day 90 | 5 pilots or contracts + $1.5M pipeline | Platform for full-scale GTM execution in Q2 |

The 90-day plan produces three things that are essential for long-term GTM success: validated ROI evidence (from pilots), validated messaging (from market testing), and a pilot model that can be replicated at scale. With these three elements in place at Day 90, the full-scale demand generation and sales motion can launch with evidence rather than assumptions.
