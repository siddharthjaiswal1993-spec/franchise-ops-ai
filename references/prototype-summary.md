# Prototype Summary: Nexus Foresight Hub

**URL:** https://nexus-foresight-hub.lovable.app

---

## Product Concept

Nexus Foresight Hub is a working prototype built on the Lovable platform demonstrating the core concepts of an AI-native franchise operational intelligence platform. The prototype was built to make the research paper's strategic concepts tangible and demonstrable in a real product experience.

The prototype name "Nexus Foresight Hub" references the convergence of multiple operational data sources (Nexus) with predictive operational intelligence (Foresight) in a unified platform view (Hub).

---

## Target Users Demonstrated

The prototype is designed to demonstrate value for three primary users:

**VP of Operations / COO:** Portfolio health view, location health scores, risk alerts, executive command center

**Field Consultant / Director of Field Operations:** Field consultant workspace, pre-visit brief, visit preparation tools, corrective action management

**CEO / PE Operating Partner:** Executive portfolio health brief, risk concentration view, board-ready metrics summary

---

## Key Modules Shown in Prototype

**Location Health Score Dashboard:** Portfolio-level view showing health score distribution across locations. Individual location health scores with dimension breakdown. Score trajectory visualization. Peer benchmarking context.

**Field Consultant Workspace:** Portfolio sorted by AI-recommended visit priority. Pre-visit brief for a specific location showing health score, open corrective actions, training completion data, and recommended focus areas. Visit preparation tools.

**Corrective Action Pipeline:** Open corrective action list with age, owner, deadline, and status. Risk-stratified view of at-risk corrective actions.

**Executive Command Center:** Portfolio health distribution summary. Top risk locations with recommended actions. AI-generated weekly portfolio brief preview.

**Risk and Alerts View:** Active alert list with anomaly detection flags and routing information.

---

## Demo Flow (DETECT -> DECIDE -> ACT -> VERIFY)

The prototype is designed to be walked through in the DETECT -> DECIDE -> ACT -> VERIFY narrative sequence:

**Step 1 (DETECT):** Open the portfolio dashboard. Show the health score distribution. Point to an At Risk location. Show the declining score trajectory and the multi-signal pattern driving the score down.

**Step 2 (DECIDE):** Click into the AI recommendation for the At Risk location. Show the recommended action (focused field visit), confidence score, source citations, and comparable cases.

**Step 3 (ACT):** Navigate to the field consultant workspace. Show the pre-visit brief generated for the location. Explain the voice-to-structured notes and corrective action auto-drafting concepts (narrative description since voice capture requires the full platform).

**Step 4 (VERIFY):** Show the outcome tracking view — health score trajectory post-intervention, corrective action closure rates, audit score recovery.

This narrative arc — from signal detection through verified outcome — is the demonstration of the platform's core value proposition and can be completed in a 15-20 minute demo.

---

## AI-Native Strategy Demonstrated

The prototype demonstrates the AI-native strategy through three specific product experiences:

**Explainability:** Every health score includes the top-3 driver explanation with source data. The AI is never a black box — it always shows its work.

**Human approval architecture:** The prototype shows the recommendation-review-approval flow, demonstrating that AI recommends and humans decide. This is the governance architecture in product form.

**Outcome linkage:** The verification view shows health score trajectory and corrective action closure rates following interventions — making the feedback loop visible to users.

---

## How to Walk Through the Prototype

The prototype is most effective when used as follows:

**As a demo to structure the DETECT -> DECIDE -> ACT -> VERIFY narrative:** Walking through the prototype in the order described above demonstrates both the strategic framework and the product thinking simultaneously.

**Important caveats to mention:**
- The prototype was built on Lovable with AI-generated data; it is not a production platform
- The data shown is illustrative, not from actual franchise customers
- The purpose is to make the strategic concepts tangible, not to demonstrate working software engineering
- The prototype represents a product vision, not an implemented product

**How to frame it:** "This is a prototype I built to make the strategy tangible — to show what I mean by 'signal to verified action' as a product experience, not just a strategic concept. It helped me identify the specific UX decisions that matter most in an operational intelligence platform."
